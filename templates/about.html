Faculty Self-Appraisal System

What are challenges of Current Faculty Self-Appraisal System?

Faculty appraisal faces several challenges. Interpretive evaluation ensures fair assessments. Bias mitigation reduces unfair influences. Multidisciplinary difficulties arise from diverse fields. Time restrictions limit thorough evaluations. Multiple roles complicate balancing teaching, research, and administration. Data professional development improves data use. Integration of technology enhances the process. Autonomy vs accountability balances independence and responsibility.

What is importance of Faculty Performance?

Faculty performance and appraisal systems are crucial. Digital platforms streamline processes. Personalized feedback enhances growth. Professional development is supported. Collaboration hubs foster teamwork. Data analytics provide insights. Open and direct communication ensures transparency.
